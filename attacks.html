Types of Cyber Attacks

Learn about the most common cyber attacks you should know.

Cyber Attacks

Phishing

Tricking users into giving sensitive information via fake emails or websites.

Malware

Software designed to damage or gain unauthorized access to systems.

Ransomware

Malware that locks your files until a ransom is paid.

DDoS

Overloading websites with traffic to make them unavailable.

Password Attack

Attempting to guess passwords to access accounts illegally.
